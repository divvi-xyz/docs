## Overview

Ordibl.ai AI-powered voice platform designed to make and receive phone calls using artificial intelligence voice agents. It integrates with telecom networks to enable automated or semi-automated voice interactions, such as customer service calls, sales outreach, or information hotlines, using virtual phone numbers.
It automates inbound & outbound calls, supports multilingual communication, and seamlessly integrates with CRMs.
