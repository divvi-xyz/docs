Prem is a powerful AI platform that gives you all the tools necessary to fine-tune and evaluate your own custom AI models.

Just load your dataset, choose a model, and in a few clicks, you can have a distilled, fine-tuned model ready to use in no time.

We designed Prem to make AI integration seamless, efficient, and effective so that you can focus on developing your product and not all the AI complexity.


### Heres what you can do with Prem:

- Access the best in class AI models without any vendor lock-in.
- Autonomously fine-tune models with your own datasets to customize for your use case.
- Evaluate your models with our built-in metrics and tools.
- Test your models with our built-in model playground.
- Keep track of your models and their performance over time.

Check out our [Quickstart Guide](/introduction) to get started in minutes.
