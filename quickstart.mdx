## How to Create an acccount on Ordibl

This comprehensive guide will walk you through the process of accessing your Ordibl Dashboard, ensuring you can quickly and securely log in to manage your account.
To create a new Ordibl account, visit the [Ordibl signup page](https://app.ordibl.com/signup) and follow the instructions to register.

<Frame caption="Ordibl Landing Page">
  <img src="/images/landing.jpeg" />
</Frame>

### Step 1: Navigate to the Ordibl Website
Open your preferred web browser and enter the Ordibl URL in the address bar. Click on the ["Get Started"](https://app.ordibl.com/signup) button to proceed to register for a new account.
<Frame caption="Ordibl Signup Page">
  <img src="/images/signup.png" />
</Frame>
### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials
In the Sign up form, fill in your name and email addres. Then input your password. Ensure your credentials are entered correctly, as the system is case-sensitive. Accept the terms and conditions by checking the box, and then click on the "Sign up" button.

<Frame caption="Ordibl Signup Form">
  <img src="/images/accept-terms.jpeg" />
</Frame>

### Step 3: Choose an Industry
Select the industry that best fits your needs. This helps Ordibl tailor its services to your specific requirements.
<Frame caption="Ordibl Industry Selection">
  <img src="/images/industry.jpeg" />
</Frame>

### Step 5: Enter your OTP
Next, you will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) via email. Check your inbox for an email from Ordibl and enter the OTP in the provided field on the website. This step is crucial for verifying your identity and securing your account.
<Frame caption="Ordibl OTP Verification">
  <img src="/images/otp.jpeg" />
</Frame>
Upon successful authentication, you will be automatically redirected to your Ordibl Dashboard, where you can access all account features and management tools.

<Frame caption="Ordibl Dashboard">
  <img src="/images/dashboard.jpeg" />
</Frame>

# How to Log in to Ordibl

## Troubleshooting

- **Forgotten Password**: If you cannot remember your password, look for the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
- **Account Locked**: After multiple failed login attempts, your account may be temporarily locked. Wait a few minutes before trying again, or contact support.
- **Browser Issues**: Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings. The Ordibl Dashboard requires JavaScript for full functionality.

## Additional Information

For security purposes, always log out when you're finished using the Ordibl Dashboard, especially when using a shared device or public computer. You can log out by clicking on your profile menu and selecting "Log Out."

If you encounter any persistent login issues, please contact Ordibl support for assistance.
